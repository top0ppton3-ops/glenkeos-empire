A complete, continuous, Fortune‑500‑grade operational blueprint

---

The system begins with identity, because identity defines access.
Identity defines what a person sees, what they can do, and what they can change.
Every action in the platform flows from identity.

Identity connects to portals, which are the controlled entry points into the system.
Each portal exposes only the functions appropriate to the identity that enters it.

Portals connect to domains, which are the functional engines of the business:
ordering, booking, menus, pricing, delivery, loyalty, payments, support, analytics, IAM.

Domains connect to data, which is structured, normalized, and governed by RLS and IAM.
Data flows into processes, which define how the system behaves under every condition.

Processes connect to interfaces, which are the screens, buttons, and flows users interact with.
Interfaces connect back to identity, completing the loop.

This is the GlenKeos operating cycle.

---

IDENTITY → PORTALS → DOMAINS → DATA → PROCESSES → INTERFACES → IDENTITY

This cycle is the entire system.
